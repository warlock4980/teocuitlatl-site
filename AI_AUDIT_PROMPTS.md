# Project Mexica AI Audit Prompts

Use these prompts with Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, or another model to get useful second opinions without pushing the project into risky financial claims.

## Context To Paste First

Project Mexica / Teocuitlatl is a public prototype website for a Mexico-inspired digital rewards concept. The current site is live at:

https://teocuitlatl.com/

Important constraints:

- This is a prototype/testnet concept, not a production financial product.
- Do not describe TOM, TEO, MIC, or any badge as legal tender, a security, an investment, or a guaranteed-return product.
- Do not imply official endorsement from the Mexican government, toll operators, tourism boards, banks, or public agencies.
- Do not claim a stablecoin reserve, redemption guarantee, or real-world payment availability unless it has been legally and operationally established.
- Audience-facing copy can be emotionally strong and Spanish-forward, but it must remain accurate and careful.

## 1. Design Audit

Please audit https://teocuitlatl.com/ as a premium fintech/cultural technology landing page.

Focus on:

- First impression and visual credibility.
- Mobile layout, navigation, spacing, and readability.
- Whether the coin/forge animation feels premium or distracting.
- Whether the medallion gallery creates desire and trust.
- Whether the chatbot feels useful without being intrusive.

Return:

- Top 5 strengths.
- Top 10 fixes, ranked by impact.
- Specific text/design changes I can hand to a developer.

## 2. Conversion Audit

Please audit https://teocuitlatl.com/ and https://teocuitlatl.com/gallery.html for conversion.

Goal:

Get visitors to understand the concept, explore the medallions, vote for favorites, and contact the team for partnerships or sponsorship conversations.

Constraints:

- No investment promises.
- No token-sale language.
- No government/toll/payment acceptance claims unless phrased as future pilot concepts.

Return:

- Stronger CTA structure.
- Suggested homepage section order.
- Better partner lead-capture copy.
- 5 campaign hooks that are exciting but legally careful.

## 3. Compliance Risk Audit

Please review the site language for legal, financial, and reputational risk.

Assume this is an early prototype for a future fintech/rewards/tourism/brand partnership project. Identify any phrases that could imply:

- Investment solicitation.
- Securities offering.
- Guaranteed profits or appreciation.
- Legal tender or payment acceptance.
- Government endorsement.
- Stablecoin redemption guarantee.
- Misleading use of cultural or national symbols.

Return:

- Exact risky phrase or section.
- Why it is risky.
- Safer replacement copy.
- Any missing disclaimers.

## 4. Technical QA Audit

Please audit the live site as a static GitHub Pages site with a planned serverless Neon poll API.

Check:

- Broken links and missing assets.
- Mobile menu behavior.
- Chatbot open/close/expanded behavior.
- Gallery top-three vote behavior.
- Whether the page degrades gracefully before the Neon API is live.
- Accessibility basics: alt text, keyboard navigation, focus states, contrast.
- SEO basics: titles, meta descriptions, canonical links.

Return:

- Bugs found.
- Reproduction steps.
- Severity.
- Recommended fix.

## 5. Spanish Market Copy Audit

Please review the product language for a Mexican audience.

Goal:

Make the Spanish copy feel premium, local, culturally respectful, and emotionally compelling without sounding like a scam, political campaign, or government-affiliated project.

Avoid:

- Excessive hype.
- Promises of wealth.
- Claims of official acceptance.
- Forced or incorrect Nahuatl/cultural references.

Return:

- Improved Spanish headlines.
- Improved microcopy for the chatbot and gallery.
- 10 short social post ideas.
- 5 partner outreach subject lines.
